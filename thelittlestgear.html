The Littlest Gear

The Littlest Gear is an app designed to allow a user to track the porgress of a car through its restoration

Often, when having a car restored it can be difficult to kepp up with the progress of the restoration without the persistant badgering of the workshop the car is at. These workshops are often unorganised and restorations can get behind schedule and progress can stall.

With this app, users can add their project to their garage, and add all the jobs required to complete the restoration. These jobs can then be completed by the workshop the car is at, along with evidence of the job including images and notes from the mechanic. This helps the user to determine that the work is being done correctly, on schedule and helps ensure that no unplanned work has been carried out.

Jobs can be added retrospectively by the user and these can then be completed by the workshop. The workshop also has the ability to add jobs for completion however, these require permission form the owner before this work can be carried out.
